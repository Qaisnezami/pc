WHY DID YOU START THE PC DOC?

We like helping people with their technical problems. Where I grew up, if the machines were broken on the farm, we didn’t eat. So I have transferred that mindset and skill to computers.

How is the PC doc Differnt from other IT Compaines?

It’s the lifelong partnership of Elizabeth and Phillip who have made the difference. Elizabeth is the business and user advocate, while Phillip has been the acute technical expert. Together, they integrate their knowledge and experience at each client’s office, correlating the user experience to the odd behaviour of the network. We get our hands dirty. It’s hard to clean the mess if we don’t know the entire mess.
